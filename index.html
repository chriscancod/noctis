      ABOUT 2AM
      Every piece was designed at 2 in the morning. That's not a gimmick — it's just when the best work gets done.
      2AM is the clothing division of Night.inc — built for the ones who are still up when everyone else is asleep. Dark luxury streetwear with real weight.
      Every piece is dropped in limited quantities. We use heavyweight blanks — 240–420gsm cotton and fleece — because we're not interested in anything that doesn't last.
      The skull, the lightning bolt, the NO SIGNAL — these aren't just graphics. They're a mindset. You're either building your own future or watching someone else build theirs.
      Designed at 2AM. Worn in the dark. Built for those who operate outside the hours.
